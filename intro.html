Our Story

Since the days of yore, CoC has been hard at work making life better. Why you may ask? It is our duty. One of our commanding tenents is the helping of the less fortunate. Chris leads by example. If even he, a god among men, may stoop to our level to pay off our student loans, ensure that we have sufficient food to get through the day, and bless our youngest sons by sleeping with them, then it is also up to us, as followers of the almighty Chris, to pay that forward.
