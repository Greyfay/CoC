Our Story

A Chris, alone, is weak. Chrises together, are strong. That is the founding principle behind our organization. Much like how the colonies banded together to fight off the colonizers during the American War for Independence, so too shall we.

Our Purpose

A Chris is, by nature, discriminated against by society. Some might call them society's least priviledged group. We're here to change that. Attend our seminars to see how the most famous Chris's of our time reached their cult-like status.

Why?

Since the days of yore, CoC has been hard at work making life better. Why you may ask? It is our duty. One of our commanding tenents is the helping of the less fortunate. Chris leads by example. If even he, a god among men, may stoop to our level to pay off our student loans, ensure that we have sufficient food to get through the day, and bless our youngest sons by sleeping with them, then it is also up to us, as followers of the almighty Chris, to pay that forward.
